Quick Routing

Choose By Need

Need a short workflow form
  • Best for: login, re-auth, status updates, reason-required flows
  • Use: createFormModal(...) or preset wrappers
  • Avoid when: the flow is multi-step or page-sized
Need messaging or confirmation
  • Best for: notices, warnings, destructive confirmations, toasts
  • Use: uiAlert(...), uiConfirm(...), createToastStack(...)
  • Avoid when: the user needs structured inputs
Need data exploration
  • Best for: flat tables, hierarchical rows, visible relationship maps
  • Use: createGrid(...), createTreeGrid(...), createHierarchyMap(...)
  • Avoid when: a simple list or summary card is enough
Need loading or empty states
  • Best for: async fetch surfaces and no-result/error placeholders
  • Use: createSkeleton(...), createEmptyState(...), createProgress(...)
  • Avoid when: the final UI needs task-specific messaging or recovery flows
